Makoola Catalogue Design

Makoola Catalogue Design

Makoola is a small, family-owned brand selling high quality clothes for children. I worked with them to create a printed and online catalogue for their winter collection. The design was supposed to go along with the photoshoot and collection theme “Forest secrets” as well as being fun and interesting both for the kid and their parents.
